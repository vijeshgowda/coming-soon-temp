Our Story

A Tale Written in Time

Eight years ago, two young souls crossed paths without knowing that life had quietly begun stitching their names into the same destiny. Greeshma and Vijesh were simply best friends then — the kind who shared laughter without reason, secrets without fear, and dreams without hesitation. Their bond felt effortless, like a melody that had always existed.

As seasons passed, friendship ripened into something deeper something gentler. Love didn't arrive with fireworks; it arrived like dawn, slow, warm, and certain. They found in each other a familiar comfort, a steady companionship, and a promise that felt older than time itself. Today, as they stand on the threshold of forever, they look back at those eight years with full hearts — grateful for every moment that led them from friendship to fate.
