```
Our Story

Two hearts,
one destiny

Some love stories are written in the stars — ours was written in the warmth of a shared glance, in late evenings filled with laughter, and in the quiet certainty that this was where we were always meant to be.

Greeshma and Vijesh invite you to witness the beginning of their forever. Surrounded by family, blessed by tradition, and celebrated with every heart in the room — this day has been a long time coming.

"ಪ್ರೀತಿ ಎಂದಿಗೂ ಕೊನೆಗೊಳ್ಳುವುದಿಲ್ಲ" — Love never ends.
```

Utsav Krama

```
Dawn
ಗಣಪತಿ ಪೂಜೆ
Ganapati Pooja

Invoking Lord Ganesha — remover of obstacles, giver of beginnings — to bless the union before all else begins.

Morning
ಕಾಶೀ ಯಾತ್ರೆ
Kashi Yaatre

The groom sets off for Kashi — only to be turned back by the greatest offer: the bride's hand in marriage.

Muhurtham
ಕನ್ಯಾದಾನ · ಸಪ್ತಪದಿ
Kanyadaana & Saptapadi

The heart of the ceremony — the sacred gift of the bride, followed by seven steps and seven eternal vows around the sacred fire.

Noon
ಮಂಗಲಸೂತ್ರ ಧಾರಣ
Mangalsutra Dharana

The groom ties the sacred thread — a golden symbol of love, devotion, and a lifetime of togetherness.

Afternoon
ಆಶೀರ್ವಾದ · ಭೋಜನ
Blessings & Reception Feast

Elders shower the couple with flowers and prayers. Followed by a grand South Indian feast — please join us at the table.
```
